Chocolate Fudge Cake

Ingredients

  • 150ml sunflower oil, plus extra for the tin
  • 175g slef-raising flower
  • 2tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1tsp bicarbonate of soda or baking soda
  • 150g caster sugar
  • 2tbsp golden syrup
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 150ml semi-skimmed milk

For the Icing

  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 225g icing sugar
  • 40g cocoa powder
  • 2 1/2 tbsp milk

Method

  1. Step 1

    2. Heat the over to 180c. Oil and line the base of two 18cm sanwhich tins. Sieve the flour, cocoa poweder and bicarbonate of soda into a bowl. Add the caster sugar and mix well.

  2. Step 2

    3. Make a well in the center and add the golden syrup, eggs, sunflower oil and milk. Beat well with an electric whisk until smmooth.

  3. Step 3

    4. Pour the mixture into the two tins and bake for 25-35 mins until risen and firm to the touch. Remove from oven, leave to cool for 10 mins before turning out onto a cooling rack.

  4. Step 4

    5. To make the icing, beat the unsalted butter in a bowl until soft. Gradually sieve and beat in the icing sugar and cocoa powder, the add enough of the milk to make the icing fluffy and spreadable.

  5. Step 5

    6. Sandwich the two cakes together with the butter icing and cover the side and the top of the cake with more icing.
